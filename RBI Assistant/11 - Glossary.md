# 11 - RBI Assistant Glossary

## Purpose

Practical glossary for RBI Assistant preparation.

## Terms

### LPT

Language Proficiency Test. Verify applicability and required local language from the official notification.

### Banking Awareness

Knowledge of RBI, banks, accounts, deposits, loans, interest, payment systems, financial inclusion, and current banking news.

### Sectional Timing

Separate time limits for sections. It prevents shifting unused time from one section to another.

### Negative Marking

Penalty for wrong answers where specified. It makes accuracy strategy important.

### Payment System

Mechanism for transferring money, such as NEFT, RTGS, UPI, cards, wallets, and other regulated systems.

### Financial Inclusion

Access to affordable and useful financial services.

## Small Details That Matter Later

- RBI terms appear in GA and banking awareness.
- Local language ability can matter after written stages.
- Computer basics should be revised through short tables.
- Speed without accuracy is not a strategy.
