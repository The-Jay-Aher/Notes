# 10 - SEBI Grade A Cheatsheet

## Purpose

Fast revision sheet for SEBI Grade A. Verify exact stream, pattern, syllabus, dates, cutoffs, and weightage from the latest official SEBI advertisement.

## Core Mental Model

```text
market failure -> SEBI regulation -> investor/issuer/intermediary impact -> limitation/current issue
```

## High-Yield Areas

| Area | Focus |
| --- | --- |
| Securities market | primary, secondary, exchanges, depositories, clearing, settlement |
| Regulation | SEBI Act purpose, intermediaries, disclosure, insider trading, takeover, mutual funds |
| Finance | bonds, equity, derivatives, risk-return, valuation basics, financial statements |
| Economics | inflation, growth, monetary/fiscal policy, financial stability |
| Management | leadership, motivation, communication, ethics |
| Current affairs | SEBI circulars, market reforms, enforcement, IPOs, mutual funds, derivatives |

## Small Details That Matter Later

- Stream-specific Paper 2 must be verified.
- Disclosure solves information asymmetry, not investment risk itself.
- Primary market and secondary market serve different roles.
- Clearing and settlement are market-stability infrastructure.
- Intermediary regulation reduces conduct and conflict risk.
- Descriptive answers need examples and impact.
- Current circulars should be revised close to exam.

## Answer Frame

```text
problem
  -> regulation/mechanism
  -> investor impact
  -> market impact
  -> limitation
  -> way forward
```
