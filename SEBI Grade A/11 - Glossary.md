# 11 - SEBI Grade A Glossary

## Purpose

Practical glossary for SEBI Grade A preparation.

## Terms

### Primary Market

Market where securities are issued for the first time to raise capital.

### Secondary Market

Market where existing securities are traded, providing liquidity and price discovery.

### Disclosure

Required information given to investors and markets so risk can be evaluated.

### Intermediary

Market participant such as broker, merchant banker, investment adviser, registrar, transfer agent, or depository participant.

### Insider Trading

Trading based on unpublished price-sensitive information.

### Clearing Corporation

Market infrastructure institution that manages clearing and settlement obligations.

### Depository

Institution that holds securities electronically and records ownership.

### Mutual Fund

Pooled investment vehicle managed according to a defined investment objective.

### Derivative

Financial contract whose value depends on an underlying asset, rate, index, or instrument.

## Small Details That Matter Later

- Define every term through the market problem it solves.
- Regulations should be linked to investor protection and market development.
- Current SEBI changes can alter examples and emphasis.
- Stream-specific terms must be added based on the chosen stream.
