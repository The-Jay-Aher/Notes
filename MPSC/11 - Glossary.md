# 11 - MPSC Glossary

## Purpose

Practical glossary for Maharashtra state-services preparation.

## Terms

### Rajyaseva

State Services examination track conducted by MPSC for Maharashtra administrative posts. Verify exact current name/pattern from official advertisement.

### Samyukta Maharashtra Movement

Movement for a Marathi-speaking state with Mumbai, culminating in the formation of Maharashtra in 1960.

### Konkan

Coastal region of Maharashtra. Relevant for rainfall, fisheries, ports, tourism, landslides, and coastal hazards.

### Vidarbha

Eastern Maharashtra region. Relevant for cotton, forests, minerals in parts, agrarian distress, and regional imbalance discussions.

### Marathwada

Region often discussed for drought, water scarcity, and development challenges.

### Western Maharashtra

Region associated with agriculture, irrigation, sugar cooperatives, and strong cooperative politics.

### Cooperative Movement

Collective economic institution model important in sugar, credit, dairy, agriculture, and rural political economy.

### Rain Shadow Region

Area receiving less rainfall because the Western Ghats block moisture-bearing winds. Important for drought analysis.

### District Collector

Key district-level administrative officer involved in revenue administration, law and order coordination, elections, disaster management, and development implementation.

### Local Self-Government

Panchayati Raj and urban local bodies. In MPSC, connect constitutional provisions with Maharashtra implementation.

## Small Details That Matter Later

- A region name in a question often hints at a specific geography/economy issue.
- Reformers should be linked to institutions and social problems.
- Cooperative movement has both development and governance angles.
- State schemes and data require current verification.
- Maharashtra examples can be used in GS, essay, ethics, economy, geography, and governance answers.
