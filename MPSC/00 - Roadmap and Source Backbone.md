# 00 - Roadmap and Source Backbone

## Why This Chapter Matters

MPSC preparation is not UPSC with a Maharashtra label. It requires state-specific causal chains, geography, administration, reform movements, regional development issues, and examples that fit Maharashtra.

## The Big Picture

```text
Maharashtra history and society -> state institutions -> regional economy -> current policy issues -> exam questions
```

## First-Principles Explanation

Cause: a state civil service exam tests both national foundations and state-specific administrative understanding.

Mechanism: connect Maharashtra's historical, geographic, economic, and political realities to governance questions.

Immediate result: answers become grounded in state examples.

Long-term impact: preparation becomes useful for both MCQs and mains-style analysis.

Next connected topic: Maratha history, social reform, state formation, cooperative movement, local governance, regional imbalance, and state policy.

| Term | Meaning | Why it matters |

## Small Details That Matter Later

- Maharashtra examples should not be generic India examples with names changed.
- Timeline traps are common in history and reform movements.
- Geography questions often connect physical features to agriculture, industry, water, drought, and urbanization.
- Cooperative movement questions require both achievement and criticism.
- State current affairs need current verification near the exam.

## Answers and Reasoning

1. MPSC tests state-specific history, geography, administration, economy, and examples.
2. They connect history, caste, education, gender, politics, and modern governance.
3. It links Vidarbha, Marathwada, Western Maharashtra, resource distribution, agriculture, industry, and policy.
4. Schemes, data, budgets, and appointments change.
5. Water, soil, rainfall, ports, minerals, and urbanization shape economic patterns.
