## Manual Review Caveats

- PDF extraction does not preserve every map, image, table, or cartoon. Use the original source PDF for visuals.
- Current examples are prompts and should be refreshed from official current-affairs sources near the exam.
- Notes are intentionally crisp; if you want book-level exhaustive notes, expand each chapter after rereading the source PDF and adding page-specific facts.
- The official syllabus layer now has a first-pass common GS/Essay/CSAT note layer, but it is not proof that chosen optional-subject notes, language-specific practice, daily current-affairs entries, PYQ tagging, or solved practice banks are complete.
