# Federalism And Local Governance

Subject: GS II - Federalism and Local Governance
Book: Constitution of India; UPSC CSE Notification; NCERT Indian Constitution at Work
Class: N/A
Priority: Very high
Source file: https://legislative.gov.in/constitution-of-india/; https://upsc.gov.in/sites/default/files/Notif-CSP-2026-Engl-060226Rev.pdf
Chapter: Functions and responsibilities of Union and States; devolution up to local levels
Page range, if available: UPSC notification PDF page 31
UPSC relevance: Both

## Quick Summary

- Federalism distributes power between Union and States, while local governance takes democracy closer to citizens.
- GS II expects constitutional design plus practical challenges: finance, institutions, decentralization, Governor issues, inter-state disputes, local bodies, capacity, and accountability.
- For Mains, federalism answers should balance unity, autonomy, cooperation, competition, and accountability.

- Union List, State List, Concurrent List, fiscal provisions, inter-state mechanisms, emergency provisions, independent judiciary.

## Topic: Cooperative Federalism

## Meaning:
- Union and States work together on shared policy goals.

## Constitutional link:
- Concurrent List subjects, centrally sponsored schemes, GST Council, inter-state institutions, Finance Commission transfers.

## Important articles/institutions:
- GST Council, NITI Aayog, Inter-State Council, zonal councils, Finance Commission.

## Topic: Challenges To Devolution

## Meaning:
- Gap between constitutional promise and real transfer of functions, funds, and functionaries.

## Constitutional link:
- Eleventh and Twelfth Schedule subjects; State laws determine actual powers.

## Important issues:
- Weak finances, irregular elections in some cases, parallel bodies, limited staff, elite capture, low capacity, weak urban planning, poor accountability.

## Prelims Pointers

- Revise 73rd and 74th Amendments.
- Distinguish State Election Commission and Election Commission of India.
- Distinguish Finance Commission and State Finance Commission.
- Learn Eleventh and Twelfth Schedule themes.
- Know GST Council and Finance Commission basics.

## Mains Pointers

- For federalism questions, use political, administrative, fiscal, judicial, and social dimensions.
- For local bodies, use the "3Fs": functions, funds, functionaries.
- Mention accountability: social audit, Gram Sabha, ward committees, local planning, transparency.
