## High-Yield Exam Notes

## Topic: Comprehension

## What UPSC tests:
- Main idea.
- Inference.
- Assumption.
- Tone.
- Scope.
- Logical flow.
- Meaning in context.
- Author's position.

## Method:
1. Read the question before the passage only if it helps focus.
2. Identify conclusion and supporting reasons.
3. Avoid outside knowledge.
4. Reject extreme options unless the passage is extreme.
5. Prefer the option most directly supported by the passage.

## Common traps:
- True but not stated.
- Too broad.
- Too narrow.
- Opposite of author.
- Moralizing beyond passage.
- Confusing example with main argument.

## Topic: Communication And Interpersonal Skills

## What UPSC tests:
- Situational judgement.
- Clarity.
- Empathy.
- Conflict handling.
- Public-service temperament.

## Good response:
- Lawful.
- Calm.
- Clear.
- Respectful.
- Fact-based.
- Citizen-sensitive.
- Avoids escalation.

## Topic: Logical Reasoning

## Areas:
- Statement and conclusion.
- Assumptions.
- Strengthen/weaken arguments.
- Syllogism.
- Cause and effect.
- Arrangements.
- Blood relations.
- Direction sense.
- Ranking.
- Coding-decoding.
- Puzzles.

## Method:
- Translate words into conditions.
- Use symbols and diagrams.
- Track "only", "all", "some", "none", "must", "can".
- Separate definite conclusions from possible conclusions.

## Topic: Analytical Ability

## Meaning:
- Breaking a problem into variables, relationships, constraints, and conclusions.

## Tools:
- Tables.
- Venn diagrams.
- Flowcharts.
- Elimination.
- Case splitting.
- Assumption testing.

## Topic: Decision Making

## What UPSC tests:
- Balanced, lawful, ethical, practical response under constraints.

## Decision hierarchy:
1. Protect life and safety.
2. Follow law and due process.
3. Protect vulnerable groups.
4. Communicate transparently.
5. Coordinate with competent authority.
6. Document action.
7. Provide grievance redressal.

## Avoid:
- Illegal shortcuts.
- Ignoring vulnerable stakeholders.
- Blind obedience to unlawful pressure.
- Public shaming.
- Overreaction.
- Doing nothing when action is required.

## Practice Workflow

1. Solve one timed comprehension set.
2. Review every wrong answer.
3. Mark trap type.
4. Rewrite the evidence line from passage.
5. Repeat after three days.
