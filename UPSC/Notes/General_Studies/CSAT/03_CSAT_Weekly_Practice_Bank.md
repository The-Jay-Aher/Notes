## Comprehension Drill

## Rule:
- The correct answer must be supported by the passage, not by personal knowledge.

## Practice checklist:
- Identify main idea.
- Identify author's attitude.
- Underline contrast words.
- Watch extreme options.
- Avoid over-inference.

## Reasoning Drill

## Rule:
- Convert verbal conditions into symbols, tables, or diagrams.

## Practice checklist:
- Mark definite facts.
- Mark possible cases.
- Eliminate contradictions.
- Avoid assuming unstated information.

## Numeracy Drill

## Rule:
- Accuracy first, speed second.

## Practice checklist:
- Write units.
- Use approximation where options permit.
- Memorize basic formulas.
- Skip time-sink questions.

## Data Interpretation Drill

## Rule:
- Read title, unit, base, and question before calculating.

## Practice checklist:
- Check whether data is absolute or percentage.
- Watch percentage-point versus percent change.
- Do not average ratios incorrectly.
- Use options to estimate.

## Minimum Safe Score Strategy

- Build a comfortable buffer above 33 percent.
- Avoid attempting too many blind guesses.
- Identify personal high-accuracy areas.
- Do not let one hard puzzle consume 10 minutes.
