## How To Convert Entries Into Notes

## For Prelims:
- Ministry or institution.
- Scheme name and objective.
- Time period.
- Funding/outlay, if official.
- Beneficiary or sector.
- Exact body/report/committee name.

## For Mains:
- Background.
- Stakeholders.
- Benefits.
- Challenges.
- Governance concerns.
- Federal, economic, ethical, or international angle.
- Way forward.

## Prelims Pointers

- Cabinet decisions often create scheme, institution, mission, and sectoral facts.
- IR events should be tagged to countries, regions, groupings, and agreements.
- Official UPSC papers should feed the PYQ error log.

## Mains Pointers

- Do not use current events as one-line name-dropping.
- Convert each event into a governance, economy, society, environment, security, or IR dimension.
- Add limitations and implementation issues after official facts are clear.
