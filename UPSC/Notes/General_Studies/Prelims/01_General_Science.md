## Quick Summary

- General Science in UPSC Prelims is concept and application oriented.
- The safe base is NCERT Class 6-10 science plus current applications in health, environment, biotechnology, space, energy, materials, and daily life.
- Prepare by understanding phenomena, not by memorizing isolated facts.

## High-Yield Exam Notes

## Topic: Physics Basics

## Core areas:
- Motion, force, work, energy, power.
- Gravitation.
- Sound.
- Light and optics.
- Electricity and magnetism.
- Heat and temperature.
- Pressure and fluids.

## UPSC angle:
- Everyday applications: mirrors, lenses, rainbow, electricity safety, household circuits, renewable energy, pressure cookers, satellites, communication.

## Topic: Chemistry Basics

## Core areas:
- Matter and its states.
- Atoms and molecules.
- Acids, bases, salts.
- Metals and non-metals.
- Carbon compounds.
- Chemical reactions.
- Fuels.
- Polymers and materials.

## UPSC angle:
- Pollution chemistry, water treatment, fertilizers, food adulteration, medicines, batteries, corrosion, greenhouse gases.

## Topic: Biology Basics

## Core areas:
- Cell structure.
- Tissues.
- Nutrition.
- Respiration.
- Transportation.
- Excretion.
- Reproduction.
- Genetics and heredity.
- Evolution basics.
- Human diseases and immunity.

## UPSC angle:
- Vaccines, antibiotics, viruses, bacteria, nutrition, public health, biotechnology, genetic diseases, vector-borne diseases.

## Topic: Health And Disease

## Concepts:
- Communicable and non-communicable diseases.
- Immunity.
- Vaccination.
- Antibiotic resistance.
- Nutrition deficiency.
- Lifestyle diseases.
- One Health: human, animal, and environmental health links.

## Answer/Prelims caution:
- Distinguish virus, bacteria, protozoa, fungus, and vector.
- Know prevention, transmission, and broad public-health tools.

## Topic: Environment Science

## Concepts:
- Ecosystem.
- Food chain and food web.
- Biodiversity.
- Biogeochemical cycles.
- Pollution.
- Climate change.
- Ozone depletion.
- Waste management.

## Link:
- Read with Environment notes and GS III environment expansion.

## Topic: Applied Science

## Areas:
- Space technology.
- Biotechnology.
- Nanotechnology.
- Renewable energy.
- Nuclear energy basics.
- Digital technology.
- Artificial intelligence.
- Robotics.
- Materials science.

## UPSC style:
- Questions often ask "which of the following statements is correct" about uses, limitations, and basic principles.

## Preparation Method

1. Read NCERT science chapters for concepts.
2. Make one-page sheets for physics, chemistry, biology, environment, health.
3. Add current applications from official sources.
4. Solve previous-year Prelims questions.
5. Maintain an error log of misunderstood concepts.

## Prelims Pointers

- Prefer conceptual clarity over high-level technical depth.
- Diagrams matter: human body systems, lenses, circuits, food chains, carbon cycle.
- Revise basic definitions repeatedly.
- Link science with environment, technology, health, agriculture, and disaster management.

## Mains Pointers

- General Science supports GS III S&T and environment, but Prelims needs sharper factual discrimination.
