# 10 - RBI Grade B Cheatsheet

## Purpose

Fast revision sheet for RBI Grade B. Official exam details must be verified from the current RBI notification PDF.

## Core Mental Model

```text
economic/financial problem -> RBI or policy mechanism -> banking/market/social impact -> limitation -> way forward
```

## Must-Verify Notification Items

- year and stream
- vacancies
- eligibility and age
- Phase I and Phase II pattern
- marks, duration, sectional timing
- negative marking
- cutoffs
- interview marks
- final merit method

## High-Yield Areas

| Area | Focus |
| --- | --- |
| ESI | growth, inflation, poverty, inequality, social sector, schemes, reports, sustainable development |
| Finance | RBI, monetary policy, banking, NBFCs, financial markets, risk, payments, inclusion |
| Management | motivation, leadership, communication, HR, ethics, organizational behavior |
| Descriptive | essay, precis, comprehension, structured policy answers |
| Current affairs | RBI reports, monetary policy, Union Budget, Economic Survey, schemes, financial regulation |

## Answer Frame

```text
problem
  -> institution/policy
  -> mechanism
  -> stakeholder impact
  -> limitation
  -> way forward
```

## Small Details That Matter Later

- Grade B is officer-level and concept-heavy.
- Assistant is a separate exam track.
- ESI needs current data and reports.
- Finance needs mechanism, not only terms.
- Management needs workplace examples.
- Descriptive writing needs practice, not last-week reading.
- RBI sources should be refreshed near exam.
